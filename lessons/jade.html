Our mission is to provide a free access to learners who are taking the subject general biology 1, anytime and anywhere.

We STEM learners put a great effort to make ILaBIO an accessible and free online resource for all STEM learners, available anytime and anywhere. It aims to present lessons in a detailed and easy-to-understand manner. ILaBIO is also designed to support teachers in delivering their lessons, especially those handling students who find General Biology challenging, particularly in the second quarter lessons 1, 2, and 3.
